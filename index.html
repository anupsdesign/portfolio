Who am I ?

Hi, my name is Anup. I currently reside in New York City. That's me you see right there looking serious. I am currently an undergrad Computer Science student. I design and build high quality responsive websites to provide an exceptional user experience.

I like learning new things. I'm constantly learning web technologies and other design related topics. I like to commit myself to something if it sparks up my interest and if I find it inspiring. This is what inspired me to get myself into the field of web design. The fact that an idea inside one's mind can be turned into an action fascinates me more than anything else.

When I'm not coding you will probably find me watching movies, drinking a cold juice or smoothie may be along with some warm MOMO (Nepali Dumpling). I enjoy a good video game. I love photography, I like to explore the city and take pictures of people and things. I might be addicted to matcha and it’s smell and the smell of air (most of the time). I taught my self HTML & CSS, but right now I’m learning Javascript along with few other object-oriented languages and I look forward to working on exciting things in the near future.

My design / development process

My design process is where all the fun begins, I never let wireframes be part of brainstorming. Anytime I get any design inspiration or idea I don't spend my time wireframing or sketching it, instead I dive right into creating prototypes using HTML, CSS and Javascript if needed. If you'd like to know more about how I work then contact me.

What tools do I use ?

I believe myself to be a quick learner, I am willing to learn new things if needed.

What I can help you with

I'll be happy to help you if you are looking for someone to help you build a clean and attractive responsive website to provide your visitors with a great user experience which may help you turn your visitors into your customers/clients. I believe that diversity of ideas makes us all wiser, so if you have an idea and are looking for somebody to collaborate with then please don't hesitate to reach out to me.
