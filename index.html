Who am I ?

Hi, my name is Anup. I currently reside in New York City. That's me you see right there looking serious. I am currently an undergrad Computer Science student. I design and build high quality responsive websites to provide an exceptional user experience.

I like learning new things. I'm constantly learning web technologies and other design related topics. I like to commit myself to something if it sparks up my interest and if I find it inspiring. This is what inspired me to get myself into the field of web design. The fact that an idea inside one's mind can be turned into an action fascinates me more than anything else.

When I'm not coding you will probably find me watching movies, drinking a cold juice or smoothie and may be eating some warm MOMO (Nepali Dumpling). I enjoy a good video game. I love photography, I like to explore the city and take pictures of people and things. I might be addicted to the smell of air (most of the time). My design process is where all the fun begins, I never let wireframes be part of brainstorming. Anytime I get any design inspiration or idea I don't spend my time wireframing or sketching it, instead I dive right into creating prototypes using HTML, CSS and Javascript if needed.
